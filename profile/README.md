# North of the Wall Productions

Welcome to **North of the Wall Productions** — we are a passionate indie game studio dedicated to crafting immersive experiences with a pixel-perfect aesthetic. Our primary focus is on **pixel art games** and **business simulation games** that challenge, inspire, and entertain players around the world.

---

## 🎮 About Us

At **North of the Wall Productions**, we believe in the charm and timeless appeal of pixel art. Our projects blend this visual style with deep, engaging gameplay mechanics — especially in the realm of **business simulation**, where strategy meets creativity.

Our mission:
- Deliver accessible, replayable, and rewarding games.
- Prioritize player agency, emergent systems, and elegant design.
- Maintain a commitment to quality, polish, and post-launch support.

---

## 🕹️ Current Focus

We specialize in:
- **Pixel art games**: From rich worlds to stylized characters and environments.
- **Business simulation games**: Create, manage, and master economic systems with depth and replayability.

---

## 🧩 Core Values

- 🎨 **Pixel Art Excellence**: Every sprite counts.
- 🕹️ **Gameplay Depth**: Systems that reward mastery.
- 🧠 **Creative Strategy**: Encouraging players to think, adapt, and build.

---

## 🔧 Development Stack

Our typical development stack includes:
- **Game Engines**: Godot, Unity (2D), and custom in-house tools.
- **Art Tools**: Aseprite, Photoshop.
- **Version Control**: Git + GitHub.
- **Task Management**: Trello, Notion.
